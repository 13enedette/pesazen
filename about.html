Mission

To empower people to have financial independence and tranquility in their senior years, by providing affordable, open, and adjustable pension plans that increase as they age.

Vision

To be the most reliable and innovative pension partner in Kenya — helping every person retire with dignity, confidence and lasting financial freedom.

Our Story

PesaZen was founded in 2025 when a team of finance and tech professionals recognised a gap: many Kenyans — especially the youth, informal workers, and SMEs — lacked accessible pension options. We bridge traditional pensions and modern fintech to deliver a simple, dependable path to retirement.
