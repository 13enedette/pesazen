Our Services & Products

PesaZen offers a straightforward suite of solutions so individuals and businesses can secure retirement with confidence.

1. Plans for Pension Savings

Flexible pension-saving plans with adaptive pricing allow members to deposit funds toward retirement at their own pace. Plans scale as income grows and are designed for long-term stability.

2. Retirement Tracking App

A mobile app that lets users monitor contributions, view personalized reports, and estimate retirement income — all in real time. The app keeps savings visible and actionable.

3. Financial Literacy & Guidance

Workshops, guides, and one-on-one advice help members understand saving, budgeting, and investment choices — giving them the knowledge to make sound financial decisions.

4. Pension Solutions for Employers

Affordable employer pension packages for SMEs that improve staff welfare and retention by offering dependable retirement benefits.
